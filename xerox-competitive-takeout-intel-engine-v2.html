How the Model Thinks

This system does not require perfect competitor confirmation. It uses internal evidence patterns — including fleet gaps, volume leakage, service friction, renewal timing, and competitor mentions — to estimate likely non-Xerox presence.

The engine combines multiple weak signals together rather than relying on a single source. Higher confidence is assigned when multiple independent signals align. Counter-signals (e.g., recent Xerox wins, stable service) reduce scores to avoid overconfident flagging.

⚠ Caution: Accounts with low evidence quality or few signal sources may produce false positives. Always field-validate before pursuing competitive displacement strategies.
